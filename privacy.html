Privacy Policy

Ball Lane Escape

This Privacy Policy explains how Ball Lane Escape may collect, use, and share information when you use the game. It is written for players, reviewers, and store visitors who want a clear summary of current privacy practices.

1. Information We May Collect

Ball Lane Escape does not require account creation or direct profile registration. Depending on your device settings, the game and its service providers may collect limited technical and advertising-related information, such as:

  • device identifiers, including advertising identifiers when permission is granted;
  • device type, operating system version, language, app version, and basic performance information;
  • approximate network or regional information derived from your connection;
  • ad interaction information, such as whether an ad request, impression, or click occurred.

2. How Information May Be Used

Information may be used to operate the game, support rewarded or other ad experiences, improve stability, prevent abuse, and understand general performance of the app. Where permitted, third-party advertising partners may also use identifiers to deliver and measure ads.

3. Advertising, Tracking, and Third Parties

The current project includes third-party advertising and related SDK dependencies, including providers such as Chartboost, Yandex Ads, Vungle, and InMobi, along with related mediation or measurement components. The app also includes logic to request App Tracking Transparency permission before using the advertising identifier.

If you deny tracking permission, tracking-related functionality may be reduced or unavailable, but you can still use the game itself.

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited technical and advertising-related information with service providers that help us run the app, deliver ads, measure campaign performance, or protect the service. We do not describe these providers as having permission to use your data for unrelated purposes beyond their own policies and applicable law.

5. Your Choices

  • You can manage App Tracking Transparency permission in iOS Settings.
  • You can manage ad-related preferences through available Apple device settings.
  • You can stop using the app at any time and contact us if you have questions about privacy.

6. Children’s Privacy

Ball Lane Escape is not intentionally designed to collect personal information from children without appropriate legal basis. If you believe information from a child has been provided improperly, please contact us so we can review the situation.

7. Data Retention and Security

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information and the third-party services involved. We take reasonable steps to limit access and support secure operation of the app, but no storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product, legal, or operational changes. The updated version will be posted with the latest revision in this file or on the relevant support page.
